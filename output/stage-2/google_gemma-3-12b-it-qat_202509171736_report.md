#### 2. Introduction and Background

Initial statements from the suspects were contradictory: Quan Xiuan claimed the money was for family inheritance and gambling debts while Joseph Prinse stated he was merely providing transport services as part of his car rental business without knowledge of the cash. These claims are contradicted by available evidence, as detailed below.
